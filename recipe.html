Simple cookie recipe

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Butter

Directions

  1. Preheat your oven to 3750C
  2. Mix all your ingredients in a large bowl
  3. Place Spoonfuls of dough In a Baking Sheet
  4. Bake for 10-12 min

Cooking terms

Preheat
Heat(an oven or grill) before hand
Bake
Cook (food) by dry heat without exposure to direct heat
